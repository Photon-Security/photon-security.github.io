About PhotonSec

Advanced Security Consulting & Research

Helping organizations navigate offensive security, cloud hardening, and AI/LLM threats

PhotonSec is a specialized cybersecurity consulting firm focused on the intersection of emerging technologies and security threats. We combine deep technical expertise with strategic thinking to help organizations strengthen their security posture against evolving attack surfaces.

Our work spans offensive security assessments, cloud security architecture, AI/LLM threat research, and security leadership advisory. We've delivered engagements for multinational organizations across the APAC and EMEA regions, with particular expertise in enterprise environments and complex security transformations.

Through our research program, we've responsibly disclosed vulnerabilities to industry leaders including GitHub, Microsoft, Atlassian, and Wiz. We publish open-source security tools and contribute to the security community through technical articles and industry collaboration.

Founded by Florian Bidabé

Agentic AI & Cybersecurity Leader

Florian Bidabé is an Agentic AI & Cybersecurity Leader with 15+ years of experience across IT and cybersecurity. Currently serving as Strategy and Innovation Lead at the Commonwealth Bank of Australia, he drives cyber security strategy and innovation initiatives in one of Australia's largest organisations.

With deep expertise spanning offensive security, cloud security architecture, AI/LLM threat research, and enterprise security leadership, Florian bridges the gap between technical execution and strategic leadership. His global career includes senior leadership roles at multinational organizations including CBA, Yum! Brands, VMware, and Apple across the APAC and EMEA regions.

As an active security researcher, he has responsibly disclosed vulnerabilities to GitHub, Microsoft, Atlassian, and Wiz. He is also a prolific security tooling developer, author of 20+ technical articles, and founder of PhotonSec.
